Rules of the game,

While baseball seems to be a homegrown American sport the original rules were based on a British bat and ball game called rounders. The Cartwright's club in New York then drafted a written set of 20 rules called the Knickerbocker rules in 1845.

Baseball as a business

As soon as people realized that fans would pay to watch the game it became a business. There have been 5 distinct eras to the business of baseball. The amateur era, the ticketed game era, professionalization, League formation, and the industrial era.
